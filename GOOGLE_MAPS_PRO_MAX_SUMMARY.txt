Arbebus PRO MAX Google-style package

Included in this package:
- current step progress in HomeBottomSheet timeline
- active step highlighting (CURRENT STEP x/y)
- automatic CTA switching based on GPS proximity to boarding/alighting/destination
- real-time CTA labels:
  - Eiti iki stotelės
  - Lauk autobuso
  - Lipk dabar
  - Išlipk dabar
  - Eiti iki tikslo
- current-step map focus from useSmartRoute:
  - walk to stop -> focus user -> boarding stop
  - ride/alight -> focus transit preview
  - final walk -> focus alight stop -> destination
- dynamic primary action opens walking navigation for walking steps
- for boarding/ride/alight states CTA collapses sheet to show the map better

Main edited files:
- hooks/useSmartRoute.ts
- hooks/usePrimaryAction.ts
- components/home/HomeBottomSheet.tsx
- app/(tabs)/index.tsx
