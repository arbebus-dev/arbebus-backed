# Arbebus Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-02

Arbebus helps users plan public transport trips, find nearby stops, see live bus data, and receive trip reminders.

## Data we use

- **Location while using the app**: used to find nearby stops, calculate routes, and show walking guidance.
- **Background location**: used only during an active trip to warn when approaching the alighting stop or to recalculate a route.
- **Notifications**: used for trip reminders, boarding alerts, and alighting alerts.
- **Diagnostics / analytics**: may be used to improve reliability and quality if enabled.

## What we do not do

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not use location for third-party advertising.
- We do not track users across other apps or websites.

## Data retention

Location is used in real time for trip functionality. If a routing request is sent to the server, it is used to calculate the trip.

## Permissions

Users can change location and notification permissions at any time in iOS or Android settings.
